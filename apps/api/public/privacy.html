Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This policy describes how botcall ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account data: email address and name provided at signup via Clerk
  • Usage data: phone numbers provisioned, SMS messages received, API usage counts
  • Billing data: subscription status managed by Stripe. We do not store payment card details.
  • SMS content: text bodies of inbound SMS messages received on your provisioned numbers, stored to enable the polling API

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and operate the Service
  • Process billing and enforce plan limits
  • Send transactional emails (account, billing) — no marketing without consent
  • Investigate abuse and enforce our Terms of Service

3. SMS Content

SMS messages received on your numbers are stored to enable the polling and inbox API. Messages are scoped to your account and not shared with other users. You may delete numbers (which stops new messages) at any time.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Clerk — authentication and user management
  • Stripe — payment processing and subscription management
  • SignalWire / Telnyx / Twilio — phone number provisioning and SMS delivery
  • Railway / Neon — hosting and database infrastructure

5. Data Retention

Account data is retained while your account is active. SMS message history is retained for 90 days. You may request deletion at any time by emailing us.

6. Security

API keys are stored as one-way hashes and never retrievable in plain text after initial creation. All data is transmitted over HTTPS.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at hello@botcall.io.

8. Contact

Privacy questions: hello@botcall.io
